## 3. 它們在做什麼:題材分布

從 408 家的價值主張歸納,IT 墳場集中在以下類型(由多到少):

| 題材類型 | 典型代表 | 為何高死亡 |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| **垂直 SaaS / 全方位營運平台** | Kyte, Lekee, Dukaan | 「all-in-one」承諾大、但每個模組都打不贏專精對手 |
| **線上教育 / 技能平台** | Pluralsight, Juni Learning, Zhangmen | 內容易被複製、免費替代多、留存難 |
| **無代碼 / 開發者工具** | Dukaan, Gogohire | 開發者期待免費開源、變現路徑長 |
| **硬科技(晶片/顯示/VR)** | HSMC, Zeku, Pico, JOLED | 重資本、TRL 落差大、地緣政治風險 |
| **雲端 / 基礎設施** | Kingsoft Cloud | 對上 AWS/阿里雲的規模碾壓,單位經濟難正 |
| **消費電子 / 手機** | Smartisan, Gionee, FCNT | 紅海硬體、品牌溢價撐不起成本結構 |
| **內容分發 / P2P 技術** | Kuaibo | 技術中立性撞上法規與盜版爭議 |

> **最大絞肉機 = 「全方位垂直 SaaS」**:第 4 節五個招牌裡有三個(Kyte/Dukaan + Lekee)
> 都是「給某垂直行業的 all-in-one 平台」,且全部死於 **Unit Economics**。
> 模式高度雷同 —— 這是 IT 創業者最該警惕的死亡模板。

### 4.1 Pluralsight — 企業科技技能的 Netflix

**USA · $4,000M · 2004–2024 · 死於 Unit Economics**
做企業開發者的線上技能影片庫,被收購後因「賣了很多席次但沒人用」的單位經濟崩潰。

**💎 The Loot(精選原文):**

> 1. *Engagement is the only metric that matters in edtech. Pluralsight optimized for enterprise sales (seats sold) rather than learner outcomes (skills gained)... A platform where 5% of users are highly engaged is better than one where 100% have access but don't use it.*
> 2. *Content creation cannot be your primary moat in 2024. Pluralsight spent millions on instructor-created courses that depreciated like fresh produce... The moat is the personalization engine, the engagement system, and the skills graph—not the content itself.*
> 3. *Seat-based SaaS pricing is dead for low-engagement products. Enterprises won't pay $500/seat/year when only 10% of seats are active... developers have infinite free alternatives.*
> 5. *Enterprise sales without bottom-up adoption is a death sentence in developer tools... Modern B2B requires product-led growth: individual developers must love the product and pull it into their organizations.*

> *(原始 the_loot 共 8 條,此處取最核心 4 條)*

**🔧 Loot Drop 重建構想 — "CodeSensei"**:把影片庫換成**活在 IDE 裡(VS Code/Cursor)的 AI 結對導師**,讓開發者「邊做專案邊學」而非看片;護城河放在個人化引擎與技能圖譜,定價改成消費/成效制,並走 product-led 由下而上滲透企業。

### 4.2 Smartisan(錘子科技)— 「中國的 Apple」

**China · $250M · 2012–2019 · 死於 Unit Economics**
名嘴創辦人羅永浩打造的「文青精品手機」,設計拿獎卻打不過小米的性價比,硬體微利吞噬一切。

**💎 The Loot(精選原文):**

> 1. **Hardware Requires 10x the Capital You Think** — *Smartisan raised $250M but needed $500M+ to survive... Software has 90% gross margins; hardware has 8-15%. Plan accordingly or don't build hardware.*
> 2. **Founder-Market Fit Beats Founder Celebrity** — *Luo was a charismatic marketer but had zero manufacturing/supply chain experience... Celebrity attracts initial customers but can't fix broken unit economics.*
> 3. **Design Awards Don't Pay Bills** — *The T1 won an iF Gold Award, but customers bought Xiaomi at 40% lower prices. In commoditized markets, design is a tiebreaker, not a wedge.*
> 4. **The 36-Month Scale Window** — *In capital-intensive low-margin markets you have 24-36 months to reach top-3 scale or be squeezed out. Smartisan was at 1M units/year in Year 4 when they needed 10M+.*
> 5. **Vertical Integration is a Trap Without Scale** — *They controlled design/OS/brand but outsourced manufacturing—the worst of both worlds... The middle ground bankrupts you.*

**🔧 Loot Drop 重建構想 — "Cortex OS"**:不做硬體,改做 **Android 上的 AI-native 系統層**(launcher + 系統 app),把錘子的生產力創新用 2024 的 AI 重做成軟體,徹底避開硬體的微利陷阱。

### 4.3 Dukaan — 印度小商家的無代碼電商

**India · $17M · 2020–2024 · 死於 Unit Economics**
COVID 期間爆紅(讓印度小店 30 秒開網店),5M 用戶卻轉換率 <3%、被低價值用戶的客服成本拖垮。

**💎 The Loot(精選原文):**

> 1. *Horizontal SaaS for micro-SMBs is a trap without embedded fintech or marketplace dynamics. Infrastructure alone (storefront, payments, hosting) is commoditized... The money is in operational workflows or taking a rev-share on GMV, not flat subscriptions.*
> 2. *Freemium models in price-sensitive markets create vanity metric disasters. 5M users means nothing if conversion is under 3% and LTV is $50. Better to have 10K merchants paying $100/month with high retention than 1M free users burning support costs.*
> 3. *COVID growth can mask fundamental product-market fit issues... When tailwinds reverse, unit economics truth emerges. Stress-test your model assuming normal market conditions, not black swan events.*
> 4. *Customer support costs scale linearly with low-value users and will kill you. If your $10/month customer requires $15/month in support, you have a services business, not SaaS.*

**🔧 Loot Drop 重建構想 — "DukaanAI"**:放棄橫向通用店面,改做**垂直 AI 營運系統**(先鎖美容沙龍與居家服務商),靠「賺營運流程/抽 GMV」而非月費,避開 commoditized 基礎建設的死局。

### 4.4 Kyte (B2B SaaS) — 印度中小企業的全方位營運平台

**India · $10M · 2020–2024 · 死於 Unit Economics**
想用整合工具取代中小企業的試算表,但在低 ARPU 市場做橫向 B2B SaaS,數學根本算不過來。

**💎 The Loot(精選原文):**

> 1. *Horizontal B2B SaaS in low-ARPU markets is a VC-funded suicide mission unless you have Zoho's 25-year head start... if ARPU is under $50/month and CAC requires human touch, you cannot build a venture-scale business. Go vertical.*
> 2. *Feature parity is not a moat in 2025. Kyte built inventory, billing, CRM, analytics—so did 50 other startups. The winners today are AI-native... If your product can be replicated in 6 months, you don't have a moat.*
> 3. *Indian SME sales cycles require founder-led, outcome-based selling, not SaaS playbooks from Silicon Valley... The founder must sell the first 50 customers personally.*
> 4. *Churn in SME SaaS is an existential threat... Kyte likely had 60-70% annual churn but probably treated it as a support problem rather than a product problem.*

**🔧 Loot Drop 重建構想 — "BakeryOS"**:**只服務烘焙坊/甜點店**這一個極窄垂直,專攻其三大痛點(生鮮 30-40% 損耗、庫存、客戶),用 AI 把通用工具做成行業專用 —— 寧可在小 TAM 當第一,也不在大紅海當第 51 名。

### 4.5 Kuaibo(快播)— 技術中立性撞上法規

**China · $0M · 2007–2014 · 死於 Legal/Regulatory**
P2P 影片技術,3 億中國用戶把它當「盜版界的 Netflix」。佔了全國 40% 流量,反而成為被開刀的標靶。

**💎 The Loot(精選原文):**

> 1. *'Technology neutrality' is not a defensible legal strategy when your product's primary use case is illegal, and you control the discovery layer. Kuaibo's P2P could have been neutral, but the centralized search/indexing servers made the company legally liable.*
> 2. *P2P architectures solve the wrong problem in the modern internet. Kuaibo's core innovation—offloading bandwidth to users—made sense when CDN cost $0.10-0.50/GB. Today Cloudflare R2 charges $0.015/GB...*
> 3. *Capturing 40% of a country's internet traffic makes you a target, not a defensible moat... in regulated markets, stay below the 'political attention threshold'.*

**🔧 Loot Drop 重建構想 — "StreamForge"**:把同樣的串流技術轉做**「影片創作者版 Shopify」**(教育/健身/利基創作者開自有品牌平台),合法變現、避開盜版原罪。
有趣的是它建議的技術棧是 `Cloudflare Stream + Mux + Supabase + Algolia + Stripe`,2-3 週可搭起 —— **連 Loot Drop 自己都在用 Supabase**(見 `docs/02`)。

## 5. 給創業者的 IT/SaaS 賽道避雷總結

綜合 408 家的死法與上述招牌的 the_loot,提煉出 IT/軟體創業最常踩的 7 個雷:

1. **「橫向 all-in-one」是死亡模板**
   低 ARPU 市場做通用平台,每個模組都打不贏專精對手。出路只有一條:**夠窄的垂直**
   (Kyte→只做烘焙坊、Dukaan→只做沙龍),在小 TAM 當第一,而非大紅海當第 51 名。

2. **功能對等 ≠ 護城河**
   「我也有庫存/CRM/分析」六個月就被複製。2025 的護城河是 **AI-native 的工作流自動化**
   與**個人化引擎/資料圖譜**,不是功能清單,更不是內容庫(Pluralsight 的內容像生鮮一樣折舊)。

3. **席次制/Freemium 在低互動產品上已死**
   賣很多席次但沒人用 = 單位經濟崩潰(Pluralsight)。5M 免費用戶轉換 <3% = 虛榮指標災難
   (Dukaan)。定價要綁**價值交付**(消費制/成效制/抽 GMV),先 qualify 用戶再放量。

4. **硬體要的資本是你想像的 10 倍**
   軟體毛利 90%、硬體 8-15%。$250M 對手機是杯水車薪(Smartisan 需 $500M+)。
   除非有 $1B+ 走完全垂直,否則別碰硬體 —— 或乾脆把它重做成軟體層。

5. **Bottom-up / 創辦人親自賣前 50 個客戶**
   開發者工具靠由下而上滲透(Pluralsight 敗在 top-down 賣給不用產品的主管);
   印度中小企業靠創辦人親自成交、磨出話術與案例,而非照搬矽谷 BDR cold-call 劇本。

6. **黑天鵝順風會掩蓋真實的 PMF**
   COVID 暴紅不是產品力(Dukaan)。永遠用**正常市況**壓力測試你的單位經濟,
   別把一次性紅利當成長曲線。

7. **規模在受監管市場是負債,不是護城河**
   佔 40% 流量讓你無法被忽視、最容易被開刀(Kuaibo)。「技術中立」在主要用途違法、
   且你掌握 discovery 層時,**不是有效的法律抗辯**。

---

> **IT 賽道一句話總結**:
> 在軟體業,**便宜起步 = 人人能做 = 競爭碾壓(64% 死於此)**。
> 活下來的關鍵不是「做得出來」或「燒得起」,而是**夠窄、夠 AI-native、夠快讓使用者上癮、
> 定價綁住真實價值**。Loot Drop 給每家開出的重建藥方,幾乎都指向同一帖:
> **垂直 + AI 原生 + 嵌入工作流 + 價值定價**。
